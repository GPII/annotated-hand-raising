Before TOHRU can be used in a meeting, the meeting has to be registered and given a unique name. The name must be unique to all meetings registered on the server. Once a name is registered it will be retained for 2 months after its last use (moderator login). If you want to preserve a meeting title please log in at least once every two months as a moderator.

Security:

If meetings are private and password-like protection is desired, simply use a longer name and/or append a unique password or passphrase to the end of the name (e.g. "ACME Monthly Meeting - 12&#G")

Participants can use the "Currently Speaking" box to see the name of the person currently speaking. This is especially helpful to those who are deaf and following by captions or participants who do not know the voices of the speakers.
This open source tool was developed by the Trace R&D Center at the University of Wisconsin-Madison to facilitate meeting participation by all users, the quick, the polite, those who use captions to follow a meeting, etc.
